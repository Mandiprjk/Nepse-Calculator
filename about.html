ABOUT US

NSBSC (Nepali Share Price Calculator) is a tool that can be used in Nepali Share Market to calculate the price regarding Buy, Sell, Broker Commission, Sebon Fee, DP Fee, etc. It provides exact amount to be received while selling any share (Stock) and the amount to be paid while buying any share (Stock) .
